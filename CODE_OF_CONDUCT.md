# Code of Conduct

Frag follows a simple rule: be respectful, constructive, and specific.

Examples of welcome behavior:

- explaining tradeoffs clearly
- giving actionable feedback
- being patient with learners
- focusing critique on code, design, and documentation

Examples of unwelcome behavior:

- harassment
- personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- repeated disruption of project discussions

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors who make the project hostile for others.

To report a problem, contact the maintainer through:

https://github.com/afnanksalal
